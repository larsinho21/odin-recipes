Grilled Tenderloin

This grilled beef tenderloin recipe is impressive enough for the pickiest dinner guests, but suprprisingly easy to make with simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 whole beef tenderloin
  • 8 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon dried thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. remove silverskin
  3. Mix garlic, oil, rosemary, pepper, thyme, and salt
  4. Preheat gas grill for 10 min
  5. Place tenderloin on the hot grill
  6. Grill about 5 min then switch sides
  7. Let tenderloin rest 15 min before carving
