Easy Pizza Dough

This recipe is very easy to make and tastes exactly like an original Neopolitan Pizza

If your looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck.This top rated recipe is super easy to make. Learn how to make the best Pizza crust of your life with just a few ingredients, find out how to shape the dough, and get our best storage secrets.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar
  3. Add flour, oil and salt; beat until smooth
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured sourface
  6. Transfer to prepared pizza pan
  7. Spread crust with sauce and toppings
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown (10-15min)
