Easy Buttermilk Waffles

These buttermilk waffles are crisp on the outside and tender in the middle.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • half a teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups low-fat buttermilk
  • third cup melted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder and salt together
  3. Whisk buttermilk and melted butter together in a seperate bowl
  4. Stir buttermilk mixture into flour mixture
  5. Preheat a waffle iron
  6. Pour enough batter into the preheated waffle iron
  7. Cook according to manufacturer's instructions
